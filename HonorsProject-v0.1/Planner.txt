-----------------------------------------------------
                *** Data Flow ***

User Input
   ↓
MoodEntry created
   ↓
Saved via FileManager
   ↓
Loaded into vector<MoodEntry>
   ↓
Passed to TrendAnalyzer
   ↓
Results (averages, triggers)
-----------------------------------------------------
              *** Algorithm Section ***

-Average Stress
* Loop through all entries
* Sum stress values
* Divide by total entries

-Average Anxiety
* Loop through all entries
* Sum stress values
* Divide by total entries

-Most Frequent Trigger
* Loop through all entries
* Loop through triggers
* Count occurrences
* Return highest count
